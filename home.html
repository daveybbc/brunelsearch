About

Brunel Search is a specialist search partner for senior engineering and research talent in speech, audio, and multimodal AI. We work exclusively with VC-backed and high-growth startups where model performance, latency, and system design decisions directly determine product quality.

Most generalist recruiters can screen for keywords. We can tell the difference between a candidate who has fine-tuned a diffusion-based TTS model and one who has only used an API for it — and so can the founders and CTOs we work with. That depth of domain fluency is what lets us run a fast, targeted search instead of a wide, noisy one.

Every search starts with a scoping call to pin down the technical bar, team context, and comp range, followed by direct outreach into our existing network of practitioners rather than a cold, generic sourcing pass.

Fees

Two engagement models, depending on how senior, specialised, or time-sensitive the role is.

Retained Search

20% fee — 1/3 upfront, balance on completion
or 15% fee, 100% upfront  ·  12-week guarantee

Best for: senior, founding, or mission-critical hires where precision matters more than speed.

  • Dedicated, priority search with deeper market mapping
  • Access to passive and hard-to-reach senior candidates
  • Rigorous shortlisting and technical calibration
  • Faster time-to-signal on complex, specialised roles

Contingency Search

25% of first-year base compensation
Pay on success only  ·  8-week replacement guarantee

Best for: well-defined roles where speed and flexibility matter more than exclusivity.

  • No upfront cost — pay only when you hire
  • Fast, flexible engagement to move quickly
  • Useful for testing the market before a deeper search
  • Fully success-aligned incentives

Included with every search

  • Direct outreach into our audio/speech AI practitioner network
  • Technical screening against your team's actual bar
  • Weekly pipeline updates — no chasing us for status
  • Compensation and market benchmarking for the role
  • Reference checks on final-stage candidates
  • Replacement guarantee if a hire doesn't work out

Retained searches are exclusive — we run a dedicated, prioritised process for that role. Contingency searches are non-exclusive, so you're free to run other channels in parallel.

Typically within 48 hours of a scoping call. Initial candidate conversations usually begin in the first one to two weeks.

Mid-to-senior individual contributor and leadership roles in speech, audio, and multimodal AI — from founding engineers to CTO-level hires.

Every engagement includes a replacement guarantee (12 weeks for retained, 8 weeks for contingency) — if the hire leaves or doesn't work out in that window, we run the search again at no additional fee.
